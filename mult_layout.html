What Causes Motion?

Force

This is anything that is capable of causing a body at rest to move and a body in motion to stop.

Basically, there are three examples of force

Contact Force

this is the type of force that is exerted or felt when bodies are toucing i.e in contact with one another.

Field Force

this is the force that is felt within a region of space without the bodies directly touching each other

Nuclear Force

this is the force that exist within the nucleus of an atom

a body will continue in a state of rest or in uniform motion on a straight line unless acted upon by an external force
